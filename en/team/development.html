ABOUT THE MEMBER

Elected Member of Development (EM)

At Zettahash DAO, elected members are selected by the community to represent their interests and uphold the DAO's governance. These individuals play a pivotal role in ensuring that decisions align with the community's collective goals and values. Elected members are crucial to the DAO's long-term success and strategic direction.

Position Overview

The Elected Member for Development is vital to enabling decentralized governance and community involvement with transparent data delivery. Utilizing advanced technologies and methodologies, the EM develops and maintains systems and services that impact ZH DAO's community members. Their role is central to creating innovative solutions, ensuring seamless user experiences, and supporting the DAO's engagement. Their contributions are essential to the DAO's success and adaptability in the decentralized technology landscape.

Role and Responsibilities

Website Development, Maintenance, and Analytics

  • Develop and maintain the Zettahash.org website and the Hashboard application, ensuring they meet the community's and investors' needs.
  • Regularly update and enhance the website to reflect the latest developments and features of the DAO.
  • Monitor website traffic and analyze data to inform improvements, ensuring optimal functionality and user experience.
  • Maintain and update websites regularly to keep them up-to-date with the latest features and security measures.

Hashboard Application

  • Develop systems for collecting and organizing data to be displayed on the Hashboard.
  • Maintain Hashboard Web3 integrations and improve user experience and interfaces.
  • Ensure the Hashboard contains all necessary features and links for investor and token-holder interaction.
  • Develop systems for investor onboarding and graphical overviews for vesting.
  • Maintain an exchange-rate database and wallet features such as backend token-balance-lookup.

Backend Development

  • Implement back-end functionality using technologies such as Node.js, Vue-cli, SQL-databases, PHP.
  • Ensure the smooth operation of server-side logic and integration with front-end elements.

Code Quality and Testing

  • Write well-designed, testable, efficient code by using best software development practices.
  • Troubleshoot and debug website issues as they arise, ensuring a stable and error-free user experience.

User Experience and Compatibility

  • Ensure cross-browser compatibility and optimize performance for a seamless user experience.
  • Achieve a Web3-style design and user experience, maintaining graphics and assets.

Content Management and Localization

  • Manage Zettahash.org landing-page and content management, ensuring SEO requirements are met.
  • Manage website translations, locale-specific styling, and layout to cater to a global audience.

System Maintenance

  • Maintain and update the Webflow content management system to support the latest features and security measures.
  • Maintain the Mattermost instance and associated server for effective communication within the DAO.

Web3 and Blockchain Expertise

  • Maintain a strong understanding of Web3 concepts such as cryptographic hashing/signing and account authentication.
  • Stay informed about the latest trends and technologies in blockchain and Web3 development.

Software Development Best Practices

  • Utilize knowledge of best practices for the entire software development life cycle, including source control management, code reviews, testing, and operations.
  • Research different software programs and maintain software documentation.
  • Implement contingency plans for system outages.

Team Management and Collaboration

  • Manage a team of developers, designers, and other stakeholders, ensuring clear communication and establishing what can be created within the timeframe.
  • Foster a collaborative environment where team members can contribute their best work and grow professionally.
  • Strong interpersonal skills enable effective collaboration with team members across different disciplines.

Professional Growth and Problem-Solving

  • Active participation in study groups demonstrates adaptability and a commitment to continuous learning, keeping him current with the latest trends and technologies in web development.
  • Exhibits strong problem-solving skills, taking proactive initiative to drive projects to completion and effectively adapting to new challenges.
